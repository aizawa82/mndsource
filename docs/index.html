Our Goals

We support patients with motor neurone disease/amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (MND/ALS) and their caregivers free of charge throughout Malaysia. We aim to educate medial professionals and members of the public in MND/ALS and raise awareness regarding the needs of people living with the disease.
