About us

MND Malaysia is a non-government organization in Malaysia that provides a platform for Motor Neurone Disease (MND) patients, caregivers and the public. It is run by individuals from a variety of backgrounds (medical, corporate, members of the public). Our aim is to raise awareness about MND and establish a support network which provides optimum support to patients and caregivers.
